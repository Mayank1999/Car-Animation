Running Car Animation through CSS.

Description:-

1. The Car is running on the road with bouncing effect.
2. The wheels of the car are rotating which gives the effect of the car moving.
3. The road is an image which is rotating in background with a particular speed.
4. The city is also an image which is rotating in background with a particular speed.
5. The background color is changing which gives the effects of dawn, morning ,afternoon, evening, dusk and night skies.
6. A sun and moon is rising and setting with color changing effects according to the time of background.
7. There are five buttons to set speeds of car into slow, moderate and fast and also to start and stop the car.
